Fantasy League History Website

https://martj42.github.io/nba_fantasy/
2017

This is a website tracking the NBA fantasy league I've played in since 2006. The main reason for its creation was that most of the websites hosting fantasy games do not keep the leagues’ histories.

By now the website is does not only keep the historic records but displays all kinds of statistics of league, which has added greatly to the enjoyment of the game.
