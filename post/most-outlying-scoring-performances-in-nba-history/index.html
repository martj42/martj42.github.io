Most Outlying Scoring Performances in NBA History

4 minutes
March 3, 2020

Shake Milton scoring 39 points yesterday was quite an outlier from his career average of 6.2. In fact, it was such an outlier, it made me wonder if it's the biggest outlier ever.

Spoiler: it wasn't.

The biggest outlier ever measured in standard deviations from the players’ career average was, in fact, Mario West scoring 13 while averaging 0.9 points per game.
